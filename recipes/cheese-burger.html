Blue Cheese Burger

Description

Blue cheese burgers aren't just basic hamburgers — they're a real treat, and the wise cook will make up a dozen or so for the freezer. If you like blue cheese, you'll never forget these burgers.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
    • 3 pounds lean or 80/20.
  • Hamburger Buns
    • 12 hamburger buns, split. Recipe can be modified to use sliders.
  • Cheese
    • 4 ounces blue cheese of your preferred kind, crumbled
  • Sauce
    • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
    ¼ teaspoon hot pepper sauce
  • Seasonings
    • ½ cup minced fresh chives
    1 ½ teaspoons salt
    1 teaspoon dry mustard

Steps

  1. Prepare the patties
    2. Mix ground beef, chives, blue cheese, salt, Worcestershire sauce, pepper, mustard, and hot pepper sauce together in a large bowl until well combined. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours.
  2. Form the patties
    3. Remove beef mixture from the refrigerator; form into 12 patties.
  3. Cook the patties
    4. When ready to cook, preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.Cook patties on the preheated grill until an instant-read thermometer inserted into the center reads at least 160 degrees F (71 degrees C), about 5 minutes per side. Serve on hamburger buns.
